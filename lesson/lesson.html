About me

I’ve been teaching Japanese online since Feburary,2017.
I really enjoy being able to meet, teach and talk to people.
I am a pretty chill person, and a bit shy :0)
I study English so I can understand how is it difficult and challenging it can be to study different language.
My lessons are not strict at all. So please don’t feel pressured.
I am not a professional teacher but I’m pretty good at finding a lesson style to suit each students needs,which is why many choose to continue taking my classes hopefully.
I cannot write all the details here so if you would like to know more,
please send a message anytime!
