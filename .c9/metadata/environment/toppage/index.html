\n (b.1995/Japan)\n
Hello! My name is Natsuko! I'm a versatile designer and a Japanese language teacher. Since I was a young child, I've been passionate about visual art and the expressive freedom it provides. The creative process has been a source of satisfaction and fulfillment for me throughout my life. \nI've always been fascinated and inspired by the diversity of character design styles and animation found across the world.\n
This interest in international expression and visual communication led me to attend Kansai Foreign Language University, where I studied English and further honed my abilities as a digital artist and web designer. \n
I am skilled in: Web design, 2D and 3D animation, Digital illustration, English Language instruction.\n
I've completed a variety of design projects, including design work for an animal hospital awareness campaign, animation and icons for YouTube videos, and personal art commissions. I also have over 580 hours of teaching experience teaching 90+ students through Italki HK Limited and private tutoring sessions.\n
I'm always looking for opportunities to to further expand my horizons and embrace new challenges, so please reach out to me if you're looking for web design work, digital illustration, art commissions, or help learning Japanese. Nice to meet you!\n
